About Me

I am not a symbol of hope — I am a consequence of fear.
The night my parents were murdered, Bruce Wayne died too. What remained was a vow. A mission.
I disappeared from Gotham to train my mind and body into something stronger than pain.
I mastered combat, stealth, and strategy — not to take lives, but to protect them.
Fear became my weapon. Silence, my ally. I strike where darkness lives.
I don’t believe in chance — only in preparation. I’m always ten steps ahead.
I built tools, armor, and a persona — but I am not the suit. I am the man who refuses to fall.
I work alone because I must. But I carry every life I couldn’t save.
Gotham is broken. I won’t let it stay that way.
I am not a hero. I am Batman.
